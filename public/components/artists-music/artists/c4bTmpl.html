CINEMA FOR THE BLIND

Dallas-based Cinema for the Blind features the songwriting brother-sister duo, Blaze and Kruz Bramlett. Known for their unique and dynamic vocal blends, they have the versatility to offer acoustic sets for smaller venues and events, as well as high energy electric sets for larger venues. Genres include alternative, dance, rock, R&B, and pop, with a fresh, soulful and energetic sound. The band's enthusiasum and love of music is contagious and their unique musical talents leave audiences wanting more.
